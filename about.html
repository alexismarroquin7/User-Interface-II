More About Me

My name is Alexis Marroquin. I am an 18 years old and from the Bay Area. I have been a full-stack web developer for 1 year. I graduated from Lambda School in the Summer of 2021. Also, I have been an audio producer for 5 years. I produce and mix intrumentals and music with vocals. Additionally, I speak three languages: English, Spanish, and Brazilian Portuguese.

My interests include, but are not limited to comedy, music, drawing/painting, and radio/podcasting. I like to listen to comedy shows like "Two Bears, One Cave" hosted by Tom Segura and Bert Kreisher in the morning when I am getting ready to start my day.

I am fascinated with learning about different cultures and foods. I watch brazilian television when I want to practice my Portuguese, but don't feel like doing to much work. My favorite TV show in Brazilian Portuguese is 'Pesadelo Na Cozinha', which is the brazilian version of 'Kitchen Nightmares'.
